About

About Name Trendz

The list of names and stats come from the U.S. Government Social Security Administration. Their data runs from 1890 until about 2 years ago.

Thank you to TWC (The Working Group) for providing a few extra icons that we used on this project.

Techie Stuff

This app was created in PhoneGap using jQuery Mobile. We also used Paul & Libby's free plug-in for Eclipse, MDS 1.2, to simplify the development process.
